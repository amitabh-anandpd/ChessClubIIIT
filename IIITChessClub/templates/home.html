About Our Club

The IIIT Hyderabad Chess Club has been at the forefront of competitive chess education since 2010. We provide a platform for students to develop strategic thinking, participate in tournaments, and connect with fellow chess enthusiasts.

Our members regularly compete in inter-collegiate tournaments and have achieved remarkable success at state and national levels. We offer training sessions, organize regular tournaments, and maintain a vibrant community of chess players.

Join Our Community

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the fundamentals or an experienced player seeking competitive challenges, our club welcomes all skill levels.

  • ♟️ Weekly training sessions
  • 🏆 Monthly tournaments
  • 📚 Game analysis workshops
  • 🤝 Friendly matches and study groups
