Tournament Rules & Guidelines

General Rules

  • FIDE Laws of Chess apply to all games
  • Players must arrive 15 minutes before their scheduled round
  • Default time is 30 minutes after round start
  • No outside assistance or electronic devices allowed
  • Game notation is required for classical tournaments
  • Respectful conduct expected at all times

Pairing & Scoring

  • Swiss system pairing for all rounds
  • Win = 1 point, Draw = 0.5 points, Loss = 0 points
  • Tiebreaks: Buchholz, Direct encounter, Rating
  • Byes awarded only for odd number of players
  • Late arrivals may receive time penalties
  • Appeals must be made within 30 minutes of incident
